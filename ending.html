Mercenary

Retirement

After countless years of blood and battle you have finally saved up enough gold to set yourself up. You leave your men with their shares and wish them well as you journey into your new life

Requirements:

  • Gold: 10000
  • Battles won: 20
  • Bartering: 50

Nation for hire

After years spent gathering and paying for the highest quality men possible. You have become more than a simple group of mercenaries. You have become an army. Through brute force alone you are able to sieze control of the kingdom and proclaim yourself the king. Not wanting to stray from yuor warrior roots you decide to offer your armies to other kingdoms in exchange for gold. You become a home for disgruntled soldiers looking for a life of gold and glory

Requirements

  • Army size: 10000
  • Battles won: 20
  • Commanding skill: 50

Ultimate challenge

Well you have done it. You have bested every possible opponent your lands can offer you. There is no warrior man or woman who can stand before you in battle and emerge victorious. Unsatisfied with your opponents you abandon your men and travel the world looking for an opponent who can best you.

Requirements

  • Combat skill: 50
  • Battles won: 20
  • Commanding skill: 40
